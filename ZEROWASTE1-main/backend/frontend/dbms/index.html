Environmental Impact

Food waste contributes to 8% of global greenhouse gas emissions and puts unnecessary strain on our natural resources.

Resource Waste

Wasting food means wasting the water, land, and energy used to produce it. 25% of our freshwater supply goes to wasted food.

Economic Loss

The average family loses $1,500 per year on wasted food. Globally, food waste costs over $1 trillion annually.
