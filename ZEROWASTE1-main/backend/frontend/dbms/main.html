The Value of Food

Every Meal Matters

One-third of food produced for human consumption is lost or wasted globally. This amounts to about 1.3 billion tons per year.

Impact on Lives

Nearly 828 million people go hungry every night. Your food donation can make a difference in someone's life.

Environmental Impact

Food waste contributes to 8% of global greenhouse gas emissions. By reducing waste, we protect our planet.
